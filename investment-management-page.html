Our Process

How we manage your portfolio

Our investment committee meets regularly to review markets, assess risks, and ensure every portfolio remains aligned to its objectives.

1

Risk & Goals Assessment

We begin with a thorough understanding of your investment goals, time horizons, income needs, and emotional relationship with risk before making a single allocation decision.

2

Portfolio Construction

We build a diversified portfolio across asset classes, geographies, and factors — selecting low-cost, institutional-quality funds and individual securities where appropriate.

3

Tax Optimization

We consider tax efficiency at every step — asset location across account types, tax-loss harvesting opportunities, and minimizing unnecessary turnover.

4

Ongoing Monitoring

Our investment committee reviews portfolios continuously, monitoring for drift, risk events, and opportunities to improve after-tax returns.

5

Quarterly Reviews With You

We meet with you regularly to review performance, discuss market conditions, and ensure your portfolio still reflects your goals and life situation.

Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Straightforward answers to what investors ask us most.

Do you use active or passive investing strategies?
+
Both, depending on the asset class and opportunity. We believe in low-cost passive indexing for highly efficient markets like U.S. large-cap equities, and selective active management in areas where skilled managers can add value — such as small-cap, international, and alternative investments.
What is your minimum investment?
+
Our investment management service is available to clients with investable assets of $500,000 or more. We also work with younger accumulators as part of a comprehensive planning relationship. Contact us to discuss your specific situation.
How do you handle market downturns?
+
We build portfolios to weather volatility from the start — through diversification, appropriate risk levels, and liquidity buffers. When markets decline, we proactively reach out to clients, look for tax-loss harvesting opportunities, and in some cases rebalance into equities at lower prices.
Can I see a sample portfolio or performance history?
+
Yes. We're happy to share representative portfolio structures and historical performance data during a consultation. Past performance doesn't guarantee future results, but we believe in full transparency around how our portfolios have performed across market cycles.
